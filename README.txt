BENARIAN — NETLIFY READY WEBSITE

1. Preview: open index.html in Chrome.
2. Publish: go to https://app.netlify.com/drop and drag the entire unzipped folder, or drag the ZIP.
3. Forms: after Netlify deploys the site, submissions will appear under Forms in the Netlify dashboard.
4. Domain: Site configuration → Domain management → Add a domain → benarian.com.
5. Replace images later by editing the image URLs in index.html or replacing assets/ben-tafreshi.jpg.

Important: Hotel photos currently use licensed-to-embed Unsplash URLs as placeholders. Replace them with your own authorised hotel photos before commercial promotion.
