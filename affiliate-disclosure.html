How BENARIAN may earn commission from selected travel partners.

Last updated: 17 July 2026

Our commitment to transparency

BENARIAN Pty Ltd provides independent luxury travel discovery, destination guidance and hospitality recommendations. Some links on this website may be affiliate links. If you click one of these links and complete a booking or purchase, BENARIAN may receive a commission from the travel provider or affiliate network. You do not pay an additional fee because of this commission.

How affiliate links work

Affiliate tracking may record that a visitor reached a partner website from BENARIAN. The partner may use cookies or similar technologies to attribute an eligible booking to BENARIAN. The partner controls its own website, prices, availability, booking conditions, cancellation rules and privacy practices.

Editorial independence

Potential commission does not guarantee a positive review, preferred placement or recommendation. BENARIAN aims to select hotels, resorts, tours and travel services based on relevance, hospitality quality, destination fit and the expected guest experience. Commercial relationships may influence which booking options are available, but they do not replace our independent judgement.

Partner links and prices

Prices and availability can change at any time. Always review the final price, taxes, fees, inclusions, cancellation terms and supplier conditions on the partner website before booking. A link or mention does not create a guarantee by BENARIAN regarding the supplier's performance.

Clear disclosure near recommendations

Where practical, BENARIAN places a clear commission disclosure close to booking links or recommendation content. Labels such as “Affiliate link”, “Partner link” or a statement that BENARIAN may earn a commission are intended to help visitors understand the commercial relationship.

Questions

For questions about this disclosure or a BENARIAN recommendation, please use our contact page.
