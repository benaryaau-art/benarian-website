Visa Guide

Current planning information for Iranian travellers visiting Bali and Thailand.

Before you travel

Visa rules depend on nationality, destination, passport validity and travel purpose. The amounts below separate official government fees from service prices advertised by Iranian agencies wherever available.

01

Check entry rules

Confirm visa type, permitted stay, passport validity and any onward-ticket requirements for your destination.

02

Prepare documents

Keep accommodation confirmations, return flights, insurance and financial evidence ready where required.

03

Confirm officially

Requirements and agency service charges can change. Verify the final amount before payment.
