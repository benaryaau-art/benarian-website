How BENARIAN Pty Ltd handles personal information.

Last updated: 17 July 2026

1. About this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BENARIAN Pty Ltd (“BENARIAN”, “we”, “us” or “our”) may collect, use, store and disclose personal information when you use our website, contact us or interact with our travel content and partner links.

2. Information we may collect

Depending on how you use the website, we may collect your name, email address, travel destination, proposed travel dates, enquiry details and any other information you choose to provide. We may also receive basic technical information such as browser type, device information, referring page and general website usage data if analytics or security tools are enabled.

3. How we collect information

We may collect information directly from you when you submit an enquiry, contact us, subscribe to future communications or communicate with BENARIAN. Information may also be collected automatically by website hosting, security, analytics or affiliate-tracking technologies when those services are enabled.

4. Why we use information

We may use personal information to respond to enquiries, provide travel recommendations, manage hotel or business partnerships, improve website content, maintain security, comply with legal obligations and administer affiliate relationships. We will not sell your personal information.

5. Third parties and overseas disclosure

BENARIAN may use service providers for website hosting, email, analytics, customer enquiries and affiliate tracking. Travel bookings are completed on third-party partner websites, and those partners handle information under their own privacy policies. Some providers may store or process information outside Australia, including in countries where our website infrastructure or travel partners operate.

6. Cookies and affiliate tracking

The website may use essential cookies and, when activated, analytics or affiliate-tracking technologies. Affiliate partners may use cookies or similar identifiers after you click a partner link to attribute an eligible booking. You can control many cookies through your browser settings, although blocking them may affect website or tracking functionality.

7. Data security and retention

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss and unauthorised access. Information is retained only for as long as reasonably needed for the purpose for which it was collected, legal compliance, dispute resolution or business records.

8. Access and correction

You may ask to access or correct personal information held by BENARIAN. We may need to verify your identity before responding. To make a request, use our contact page.

9. Privacy complaints

If you believe your personal information has been mishandled, contact BENARIAN through the contact page and provide enough detail for us to investigate. We will review the concern and respond within a reasonable period. You may also have the right to contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner.

10. External websites

This website links to travel providers and other external websites. BENARIAN is not responsible for the privacy or security practices of those websites. Review each provider's privacy policy before supplying personal or payment information.

11. Updates

We may update this policy as BENARIAN’s services, technology or legal obligations change. The latest version will be published on this page with a revised date.
