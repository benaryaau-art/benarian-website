Privacy Policy

Clear information about how BENARIAN operates and protects visitors.

Last updated: 17 July 2026

Information we may collect

Information submitted through forms, such as name, email, travel preferences and enquiry details; basic technical information such as browser type and pages visited; and affiliate click information supplied by partners.

How we use information

To respond to enquiries, improve the website, provide travel-related information, measure partner-link performance, maintain security and meet legal obligations.

Cookies and third parties

Booking partners, analytics services and social platforms may use cookies or similar technologies under their own privacy policies. BENARIAN does not control partner checkout systems.

International disclosure

Travel and technology partners may process information outside Australia. We take reasonable steps to select reputable providers, but their practices are governed by their own terms.

Access, correction and complaints

You may request access to or correction of personal information held by BENARIAN, or make a privacy complaint, by emailing hello@benarian.com.

Security and retention

We use reasonable safeguards and retain information only as long as needed for business, legal and security purposes.
