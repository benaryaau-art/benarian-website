Privacy Policy

BENARIAN respects your privacy. We may collect information you choose to provide through enquiries, account forms or concierge requests, together with basic website analytics needed to operate and improve the website. We do not sell personal information.

When you follow a hotel, flight, experience or other booking link, you may be transferred to an approved third-party partner. That provider processes searches, availability, payments and bookings under its own privacy policy and terms. BENARIAN does not receive or store your partner-account password or payment-card details.

Member access currently uses browser-session storage to control display of private website content. Closing the session or signing out removes that local access flag. A future secure membership provider may apply its own privacy and security terms.

Selected booking links are active. Prices, availability and booking conditions are supplied by the relevant partner and may change without notice.
