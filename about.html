OUR STORY

Luxury travel, shaped by true hospitality.

BENARIAN is a luxury travel and hospitality platform created to connect discerning travellers with exceptional hotels, refined experiences and trusted destination guidance.

Founded by Ben Tafreshi and shaped by more than 20 years of international hospitality experience across Australia, Asia and the Middle East, BENARIAN combines professional hotel knowledge with a personal, guest-first approach.
