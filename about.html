ABOUT BENARIAN

Luxury travel, shaped by genuine hospitality.

Founded by Ben Tafreshi

BENARIAN is an Australian luxury travel and hospitality platform connecting discerning travellers with exceptional destinations, carefully selected hotels, resorts and premium experiences.

Built on quality, trust and international hospitality expertise, BENARIAN offers a refined approach to travel for guests who value comfort, service, authenticity and memorable experiences.

EXPERIENCE · LEADERSHIP · EXCELLENCE

Built from inside the hotel world

Founded by Ben Tafreshi, BENARIAN reflects more than two decades of international experience across hotel operations, guest experience, rooms division, food and beverage and hospitality leadership.

Experience across Australia, Asia and the Middle East has shaped a strong operational understanding of what creates memorable stays, trusted service and lasting guest relationships.

BENARIAN brings together hospitality knowledge, strategic insight and a genuine passion for excellence to create more thoughtful and inspiring travel experiences.

Our mission is simple: deliver exceptional journeys built on trust, quality and a genuine passion for hospitality.
Where refined travel meets genuine hospitality — every journey is selected with purpose, care and an uncompromising commitment to excellence.
